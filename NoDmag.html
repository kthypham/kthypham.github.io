NōD Magazine

Art Director/Editor

NōD Magazine has history on the university campus, est. 2006 by a group of wayward undergrads. In 2015 it was disbanded when funding ran out. With four others I helped revive the publication in 2016. In doing so I also modernized the publication and its marketing materials, contributing to a successful Kickstarter campaign and grant proposal that will help keep the publication funded for at least five more years.
